Work and Wealth

The only part of the movie that relates to work and wealth is the outsourcing of the call center for the cell phone company. We see that when Lt. Colonel Lennox uses a borrowed cell phone to get in contact with the Pentagon that he first must go through a call center to pay for his call. While this is mostly added for comedic relief it does reflect the state of the world where call centers have been outsourced to India. If the call center was in the United States it is more likely that the call would have been put through to the Pentagon without requiring a credit card making it faster to get help and possibly saving lives. Besides this reference there was little comment about work and wealth within the movie.
