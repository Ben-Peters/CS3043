Chapter 8: Computer Reliability

While computer and network security is crucial, itâ€™s reliability is just as important. While many device malfunctions are trivial, the movie highlights how disastrous one of these malfunctions can be. The first example of this was when the cyber attacks left the pentagon and other government buildings in a blackout, impeding them from using traditional forms of communication. While these targeted attacks are based in science fiction, the government should have stress tested the system and had a better backup plan for if communication went down.

Another example of issues with computer reliability in the movie was the entire system keeping Megatron frozen in the Hoover Dam.

  • Generators aren't enough to keep megatron frozen. Whole system failed and he unfroze
  • Testing to find vulnerabilities in a system was not done in certain cases
